Mongolian Chicken

Mongolian chicken is a popular dish featuring tender, crispy chicken coated in a flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and other seasonings. It's often served with rice or noodles.

Fresh Corn Chowder with Basil and White Fish

Fresh Corn Chowder with Basil and White Fish is a creamy soup featuring sweet corn, delicate white fish, and fragrant basil.

Brazilian Fish Stew
